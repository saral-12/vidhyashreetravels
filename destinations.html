Kedarnath & Badrinath

The sacred Char Dham temples nestled amidst majestic snow-covered peaks. Stand before the ancient shrines of Lord Shiva and Lord Vishnu on our highly requested devotional group yatra.

Experience the majestic kingdom of Bhutan. Visit the hanging cliffside Tiger's Nest Monastery in Paro, walk through peaceful valleys, and learn the rich cultural history of Thimphu.
