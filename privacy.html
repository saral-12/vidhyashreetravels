Data Privacy

PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we handle and protect your personal details.

Information Collection & Usage

Vidhyashree Travels respects your privacy. Personal information collected through our inquiry forms, contact submission forms, or booking channels (such as full name, email address, phone numbers, and travel dates) is used exclusively for providing travel-related quotes, planning tours, and improving our customer experience.

Data Protection & Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law to process your tickets, hotel bookings, or local transport reservations with our trusted travel operators.

Security

We implement appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. Local storage data is preserved on your device and only sent to our servers when you intentionally submit an inquiry request.

Last updated: June 2026. For any questions regarding our policy, reach us at info@vidhyashreetravels.com.
