ABOUT VIDHYASHREE TRAVELS

A trusted travel partner based in Bhilai, Chhattisgarh, specialized in designing well-planned domestic and international tours with premium comforts and personalized services.

Our Mission & Values

Vidhyashree Travels is a leading tour and travel agency committed to creating unforgettable travel memories. We organize well-planned group tours across beautiful regions in India and outstanding destinations globally, keeping customer safety, comfort, and satisfaction as our topmost priorities.

One of our major specialties that sets us apart is providing **100% Pure Vegetarian Meals** during our group tours. We understand the culinary requirements of vegetarian travelers and ensure delicious, clean, and authentic vegetarian meals throughout the entire journey.

Why Choose Us?

  • Domestic & International Tours: Carefully structured itineraries covering both Indian heritage and foreign locations.
  • 100% Pure Vegetarian Meals: Freshly prepared, delicious vegetarian food served in group tours.
  • Customized Holiday Packages: Tell us your travel bounds and we will design a custom itinerary for you.
  • End-To-End Booking: Seamless hotel, flight ticket, and local transportation arrangement.
  • Experienced Tour Management: Attentive, friendly, and expert coordinators accompanying the groups.
